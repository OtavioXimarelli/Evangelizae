# Evangelizae — Manifesto, Mission, and Project Goals

> **Status:** Living Project Guide Document  
> **Version:** 1.0  
> **Core Identity:** Digital Missionary Platform for Catholic Truth and Daily Prayer Life.  
> **Languages:** Portuguese (`pt` — Primary) | English (`en` — Secondary)

---

## 1. Our Mission

**To help Catholics live their faith every day through prayer, formation, communion, and active participation in the Church.**

Evangelizae does not exist to replace the Church, the priest, spiritual direction, or the parish community. It exists to be a **daily companion for Christian life**. Every single feature must lead the user closer to Christ, to the sacraments, and to physical parish life.

---

## 2. Our Vision

To become the leading digital Catholic platform in the Portuguese-speaking world (with a global English expansion), offering a complete and integrated experience for prayer, formation, and communion, **always strictly grounded in the Magisterium of the Catholic Church**.

---

## 3. Product Philosophy

1. **Technology in Service of Faith (and Invisible)**
   * We do not aim to trap user attention or increase screen time through addictive design.
   * Our goal is to help users pray and develop a rock-solid spiritual routine. The less time a user needs to spend inside the application to complete their spiritual duties and head to physical Church, the better.
2. **Uncompromising Fidelity to the Magisterium**
   * Every answer, teaching, reflection, or AI interaction must be solidly anchored in official authoritative sources:
     * *Holy Bible*
     * *Catechism of the Catholic Church (CCC)*
     * *Code of Canon Law*
     * *Official Documents of the Holy See and the Vatican*
     * *Episcopal Conferences (e.g., USCCB, CNBB)*
     * *Saints and Doctors of the Church*
   * **Never** provide personal opinions, unverified revelations, or theological speculation without ecclesial approval.
3. **A Healthy and Fraternal Community**
   * **What we will NOT have:** addictive engagement algorithms, infinite feeds, like-chasing, excessive gamification, or sensationalist content.
   * **What we want to foster:** intercessory prayer, charity, fraternity, communion, and sacred stillness.
4. **No Competition (Personal Progress Only)**
   * In Evangelizae, there are no leaderboards, rankings, or spiritual competitions.
   * The user profile displays exclusively their **personal progress**: consecutive days of prayer (streaks), completed rosaries, daily readings, and finished spiritual plans.
5. **Spiritual Growth as our Core Filter**
   * Before building any new feature, we must answer: *How does this help someone grow spiritually, pray more, and draw closer to the sacraments and the Church?* If the answer is not a clear "yes", the feature does not belong in Evangelizae.

## 4. The 4 Pillars of Evangelizae

### 🙏 Pillar 1: Prayer (Building the Daily Habit)
* **Interactive Guided Rosary (Our Preserved Crown Jewel)**: Step-by-step prayer engine with visual beads, day-specific mystery contemplation, and bilingual (Portuguese/Latin) prayers.
* **Divine Mercy Chaplet and Novenas**
* **Traditional Catholic Prayer Library**
* **Spiritual Journal & Daily Examination of Conscience**
* **Prayer Timer & Personal History**
* **Gentle Prayer Reminders**

### 📖 Pillar 2: Formation (Accessible Catholic Teaching)
* **Holy Bible & Searchable Catechism**
* **Daily Liturgy**: Gospel, First/Second Readings, Responsorial Psalm, and theological reflections.
* **Saint of the Day & Church History**
* **Official Magisterial Documents & Deep-Dive Courses**
* **Catholic AI (Magisterium Assistant)**: Theological Q&A strictly grounded with mandatory citations from authoritative sources.

### 🤝 Pillar 3: Communion (Connecting Souls via Prayer)
* **Intercessory Prayer Wall**: Users submit intentions and others press *"🙏 I prayed for you"* (No public comments in MVP to ensure serenity and prevent debate).
* **Testimonies of Graces Received**
* **Small Groups & Parish Spiritual Missions**

### ⛪ Pillar 4: Church (Bridging Digital to Physical)
* **Mass & Confession Schedule Directory**
* **Eucharistic Adoration Timetables**
* **Parish Events, Ministries, and Retreats**

## 5. Roadmap & Delivery Phases

### Phase 0 — Foundation (Completed / Refinement)
* Manifesto, Branding, Logo, **Modern Cathedral Design System** (Vatican White & Marian Midnight palettes), Decoupled Architecture (Next.js 15 + Java 25 Spring Boot 4).

### Phase 1 — Prayer Core (Current MVP Focus)
* **Goal:** The user can pray daily and maintain a spiritual routine using solely Evangelizae.
* **MVP Deliverables:**
  1. **Sanctuary (Main Dashboard)**: Consolidated view of the 30-Day Spiritual Plan, Today's Gospel, Saint of the Day, Rosary shortcut, and community intentions.
  2. **Complete Guided Rosary**: Preserving and enhancing the interactive engine (`buildRosarySequence`), with automatic calculation of the Mysteries of the Day (*Rosarium Virginis Mariae*).
  3. **Daily Liturgy**: Clean, reverent reading of the Word of God.
  4. **Prayer Intentions (Community MVP)**: Intercessory wall with the charitable action *"🙏 I prayed for you"*.
  5. **30-Day Spiritual Plan**: Onboarding commitment tailored to user goals (e.g., *I want to build a daily prayer habit*).
  6. **Catholic AI Formation**: Chatbot strictly constrained to official magisterial documents.
  7. **Personal Progress Profile**: Daily prayer streak tracking without leaderboards.

### Phase 2 — Solid Formation (Next Cycle)
* Complete Bible, Semantic Catechism search, Expanded Novena library, Unified global search.

### Phase 3 — Community & Parish Ecosystem
* Parish intercession groups, moderated testimonies, and intelligent intercessory notifications.

### Phase 4 & 5 — Advanced AI (Catholic RAG) & Native Mobile (Android/iOS)
* Vector database integration of all Catholic Magisterium (Spring AI + MongoDB Atlas Vector Search) and publication of native mobile apps with full offline capabilities.

---

## 6. Market Positioning
Evangelizae **does not compete** with secular social networks (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook). It is not merely a standalone "Bible app" or a disconnected "Bead counter." Evangelizae is a **comprehensive digital missionary ecosystem that accompanies Catholics throughout the entire week toward holiness and physical Church participation**.
